## Communication Protocols

### Meeting Structure

#### Daily Standups (9:00 AM EAT)
**Duration**: 15 minutes  
**Participants**: All development team members  
**Format**: Async-first with optional sync discussion  

**Agenda**:
- Previous day accomplishments
- Current day priorities
- Blockers and dependencies
- Quick wins and celebrations

#### Sprint Planning (Bi-weekly, Mondays 10:00 AM EAT)
**Duration**: 2 hours  
**Participants**: Development team, Product Owner, Scrum Master  

**Agenda**:
- Sprint goal definition
- Backlog refinement and estimation
- Capacity planning
- Risk identification and mitigation
- Sprint commitment

#### Sprint Retrospectives (Bi-weekly, Fridays 2:00 PM EAT)
**Duration**: 1 hour  
**Participants**: Development team, Scrum Master  

**Agenda**:
- What went well
- What could improve
- Action items for next sprint
- Process improvements
- Team building activities

**Duration**: 1 hour  
**Participants**: Technical team members  

**Agenda**:
- Architecture discussions
- Code quality reviews
- Performance analysis
- Security considerations
- Technical debt prioritization

### Decision Authority Matrix

### Escalation Procedures

#### Level 1: Team Resolution
**Timeframe**: 24 hours  
**Participants**: Team members directly involved  
**Process**: Direct discussion and collaborative problem solving

#### Level 2: Senior Staff Involvement
**Timeframe**: 48 hours  
**Participants**: Senior developer or team lead  
**Process**: Technical guidance and decision support

#### Level 3: Director Escalation
**Timeframe**: 72 hours  
**Participants**: Relevant director  
**Process**: Strategic guidance and resource allocation

#### Level 4: Executive Decision
**Timeframe**: 1 week  
**Participants**: CEO involvement  
**Process**: Final authority and strategic alignment

### Conflict Resolution

#### Technical Disputes
1. **Documentation**: Record different viewpoints
2. **Research**: Gather evidence and best practices
3. **Prototype**: Build minimal viable solutions
4. **Review**: Team evaluation and feedback
5. **Decision**: Senior technical leadership choice
6. **Implementation**: Agreed solution development
7. **Retrospective**: Learn from the process

#### Process Conflicts
1. **Discussion**: Open dialogue about concerns
2. **Mediation**: Senior staff or director involvement
3. **Solution**: Collaborative process improvement
4. **Trial**: Test new approach for defined period
5. **Evaluation**: Measure effectiveness
6. **Adoption**: Implement successful changes
